Issuers

Why should you invest in security tokens?

And there are many more reasons to issue a blockchain-based security over a paper share certificate.

Lower fees

Legacy financial systems have countless middlemen and rent-seekers. Security Tokens, by taking advantage of blockchain technology and a fully digital infrastructure, can decrease and even remove many of the typical fees associated with capital markets, compliance, and securities trading.

Programmable securities

Programmatic transfer restrictions to maintain regulatory compliance. Automated dividend payments. Bonuses for your investors based on specific criteria. These are just a few of the benefits in using security tokens over legacy share certificates.

24/7 markets

There are no holidays, weekends, or bank closures on the blockchain. Give your investors the 24/7/365 access they want.

Automated Cryptocurrency Capital Raising

Raise funds in cryptocurrency, with your investors automatically receiving the correct number of tokens. No more bank wires, 3-5 business days, and spreadsheets keeping track of capital raised.

Liquidity for private securities

Security tokens represent a massive breakthrough in liquidity for private securities. With programmatic ownership restrictions, you can sell private securities while while allowing a secondary market to develop.
